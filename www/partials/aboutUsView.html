About

Bright Beginnings leads the design, delivery, and evaluation of innovative programs to promote the healthy development of children in the first 3 years of life.

Mission

To provide a bright beginning for all Colorado children by helping families and caregivers support their children's physical, emotional and intellectual development during the critical first three years of life.

Description

Colorado Bright Beginnings is a statewide nonprofit organization that unleashes the power of parenting for all parents, soon to be parents and caregivers of infants and toddlers.

We offer parenting education and support through individualized, interactive dialogue with highly trained volunteers and community partners in three ways: home, group and agency visits. Our research based information, guides and games designed to be used every day, provide answers to questions and concerns that parents often don't even know to ask.

Because 85% of brain development occurs in the first 3 years of life and because babies don't come with instruction manuals, we share these vital resources to help all parents feel more confident and to help build a nurturing and learning relationship with their child, creating the best chance for success in school and life while creating healthier Colorado communities.
