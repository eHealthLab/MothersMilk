About

Mothers’ Milk Messaging (M3) is a texting breastfeeding support intervention aimed at increasing exclusive and continuous breastfeeding, providing helpful links to online resources.

Mission

To adapt and program a bilingual English-Spanish tailored texting message program to support exclusive and continuous breastfeeding starting in pregnancy with the intent to breastfeed and continuing postpartum. At the same time, we hope to create a valuable resource for sharing information and social support connecting new mothers through Facebook.

Description

Mothers’ Milk Messaging (MMM) is newly developed program with content based on published work by Dr. Maya Bunik, pediatrician, breastfeeding expert and author of Breastfeeding Telephone Triage and Advice book. Targeted outcomes are maternal satisfaction with achieving feeding goals and rates of exclusive breastfeeding and duration.

Breastfeeding prevents an array of chronic illness for both mother (breast and ovarian cancer) and baby (respiratory and gastrointestinal infections, diabetes, intestinal disease, necrotizing enterocolitis, allergies, asthma, obesity and cancer) We hope to decrease mother’s choosing formula by providing encouragement and useful information during the critical period when milk supply is established.
